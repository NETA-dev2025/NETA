Interpretation Guide
  • Clustering: Samples that cluster together have similar gene expression profiles
  • Separation: Groups that separate along PC axes differ in the genes that contribute most to that PC
  • Outliers: Samples far from others may have technical issues or represent distinct biological states
  • Variance: Higher PC1 variance indicates the primary source of variation in the dataset
