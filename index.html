Pan-Neuroendocrine Tumor Atlas

A Comprehensive Transcriptomic Resource for NET Research

NETA integrates multi-platform transcriptomic data including bulk RNA-seq, single-cell RNA-seq, and microarray profiling to enable comprehensive analysis of neuroendocrine tumors across diverse tissue origins and disease states.

Data Platforms

Multi-Platform Data Integration

Comprehensive transcriptomic profiling across three major technology platforms

Bulk RNA-seq

Bulk RNA Sequencing

High-throughput sequencing data providing comprehensive gene expression profiles from tissue samples. Ideal for differential expression analysis and pathway studies.

  • DESeq2-ready count matrices
  • Normalized expression data
  • Clinical annotations
scRNA-seq

Single-Cell RNA-seq

Single-cell resolution transcriptomic data revealing cellular heterogeneity and rare cell populations within neuroendocrine tumors.

  • Cell-type annotations
  • UMAP/t-SNE coordinates
  • Marker gene lists
Microarray

Microarray Profiling

Legacy microarray data providing historical context and validation datasets for multi-platform comparative analyses.

  • Normalized intensities
  • Quality control metrics
  • Platform annotations
